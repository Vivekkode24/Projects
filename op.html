Overview:

One Piece is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Eiichiro Oda. It has been serialized in Shueisha's
shōnen manga magazine Weekly Shōnen Jump since July 1997, with its individual chapters compiled into 101 tankōbon
volumes as of December 2021.
