Dr.Stone
進撃の巨人

Overview:

Dr. Stone is a Japanese manga series written by Riichiro Inagaki and illustrated by the South Korean
artist Boichi. It has been serialized in Shueisha's Weekly Shōnen Jump since March 2017, with its
chapters collected in twenty-four tankōbon volumes as of January 2022.
