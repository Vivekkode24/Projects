Bungou Stray Dogs
文豪ストレイドッグス

Overview:

Bungo Stray Dogs is a Japanese manga series written by Kafka Asagiri and illustrated by Sango Harukawa,
which has been serialized in Kadokawa Shoten's seinen manga magazine Young Ace since 2012. The series
follows the members of the "Armed Detective Agency" as they try to protect Yokohama.
