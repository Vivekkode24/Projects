Shinobi-Future Diary

Future Diary
進撃の巨人

Overview:

Future Diary is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Sakae Esuno. It was serialized in
Kadokawa Shoten's Shōnen Ace between January 2006 and December 2010, and has been collected into 12
tankōbon volumes.
