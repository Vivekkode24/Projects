My Hero Academia
進撃の巨人

Overview:

My Hero Academia is a Japanese superhero manga series written and illustrated by Kōhei Horikoshi.
The story follows Izuku Midoriya, a boy born without superpowers in a world where they have become
commonplace, but who still dreams of becoming a superhero himself.
